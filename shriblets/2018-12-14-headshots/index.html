The Firebird Effect

A young trainer’s pokemon dies in battle, launching an investigation that takes her deeper into the shadows of Kalos—and her own psyche.

TFE is a Nuzlocke-inspired comic based loosely on Pokemon XY, launched June 6, 2016 and noncommittally canceled in early 2018. While I don’t regret starting it, it was very ambitious (especially for my first dedicated comic project), and development only got messier as time passed.

I’d like to make a majority of the story public anyway, even if it’s in summary format. So far, the character profiles for a former side character and the protagonist are the most complete writeups, and I dream of having the motivation to scrounge up enough notes and doodles to fill in the rest. You’re welcome to ask me about basically anything; the spoiler filter is completely off. ;V

A rewrite isn’t out of the question, but isn’t actively being worked on nor do I have plans to. But I’m still attached to various aspects of this story; whether they get recycled into other projects or culminate in a ground-up redo, we’ll see.
